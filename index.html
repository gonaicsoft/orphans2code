Welcome to the ‘ORPHANS 2 CODE’ Community

Orphans2Code

Orphans2Code is a not-for-profit community development project which is targeted at harnessing talents among teenagers in the orphanages and less privileged by teaching them how to code using project based learning systems, in order to give them a place in the ever improving world of technology.

We teach students ages 7-19years how to code, through our immersive free code classes, workshops, events, and after school programs. We also help our students secure remote jobs, summer jobs and internship opportunities.

We are a group of smart, dedicated professionals who share a passion for inspiring young people to realize how fun and rewarding computer science can be, and how it can open doors to amazing careers and even solve big societal problems.
